About Team Blue

We are committed to enhancing the UAlbany campus experience through our Campus Service Rating platform. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly interface for students, faculty, and staff to share their experiences and improve campus services as well.

Scope

  • In scope: Current UAlbany campus services only
  • Out of scope: Future or proposed services

How it works

  • Search and filter campus services
  • View service details and average ratings
  • Submit your own reviews and ratings
  • Help improve campus services through feedback

Disclaimer

The information and ratings provided on this platform are based on user feedback and are intended for informational purposes only.
